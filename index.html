Artisan

Traveling and exploring new cities can be very fun and rewarding! However, planning your trip is oftentimes a huge hassle. It is time consuming to research which places you want to visit, tour guides are expensive, and learning about local culture through online research is boring.

At Artisan, we believe the BEST way to explore and learn about a new city is through a challenge. We are looking to restructure the way we travel by providing a unique scavenger hunt challenge for your destination.

But there's more… as you embark in your scavenger hunt adventure, you will be able to learn about the places you are visiting without having to look away.

Utilizing augmented reality technologies, you will have all the information you need right at your fingertips without being pulled away from you live experience.
